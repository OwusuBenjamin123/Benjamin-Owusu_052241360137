What we do over here

The Faculty of Applied Science encompasses a diverse array of disciplines that bridge theoretical knowledge with practical application. This faculty is at the forefront of innovation, providing students with the skills and expertise needed to tackle real-world challenges in fields such as engineering, technology, health sciences, and environmental studies..

Within the Faculty of Applied Science, students engage in hands-on learning experiences, conducting experiments, designing projects, and collaborating with industry partners. Whether they're developing new sustainable energy solutions, designing cutting-edge medical devices, or implementing advanced algorithms for data analysis, students in this faculty are actively involved in addressing pressing issues facing society..

Why we are the best

Applied science programs emphasize hands-on learning and practical application of knowledge. This approach prepares students to immediately contribute to industries and communities upon graduation

Applied science disciplines are closely aligned with industry needs, ensuring that graduates possess skills and knowledge that are in high demand in the job market.
